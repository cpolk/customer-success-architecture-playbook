# Customer Success Architecture Playbook

This repository is a practical example of how a Customer Success Architecture team can partner with enterprise customers to drive adoption, reduce risk, and deliver measurable business outcomes.

## Purpose
Customer success architecture sits at the intersection of business goals, developer workflows, security requirements, and platform capabilities. This repo shows how to structure that work in a repeatable way.

## What’s included
- **Executive summary**: how to communicate value to technical and business stakeholders
- **Adoption framework**: phased approach for onboarding, expansion, and maturity
- **Success metrics**: examples of measurable outcomes tied to platform adoption
- **Operating model**: roles, engagement patterns, and governance
- **Escalation playbook**: a method for handling technical blockers and executive visibility
- **Sample adoption plan**: example for a complex enterprise customer

## Example customer outcomes
- Reduced lead time for change through CI/CD standardization
- Improved pull request quality and review consistency
- Better dependency and supply chain visibility
- Faster remediation of security findings
- Improved developer onboarding and self-service enablement

## Why this matters
Technical customer leaders need to connect platform features to business results. This repository is designed to demonstrate that translation clearly and concretely.
